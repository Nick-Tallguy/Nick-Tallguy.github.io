iD editor - 複製、貼上、旋轉
============

> Updated 2019-06-24

![copy-paste][]


你可以輕鬆地複製、貼上及旋轉物件，但請留意保持物件的確切尺寸。  
Select with your mouse, then **Ctrl+c** to copy, and **Ctrl+v** to paste. If you need to you can 'reflect' an object along either its long or its short axis.
